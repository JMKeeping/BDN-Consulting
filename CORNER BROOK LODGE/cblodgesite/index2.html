Welcome

As the Master of Corner Brook Lodge #11 on the registry of the Grand Lodge of Ancient Free and Accepted Masons of Newfoundland & Labrador, it is my privilege to welcome you to our website. This website is still under construction but we are pleased with the progress of its development so far.

Our Lodge was consecrated on October 4th, 1926, as Corner Brook Lodge #4832 under the jurisdiction of the United Grand Lodge of England. With the formation of the Grand Lodge of Newfoundland and Labrador in 1997, our Lodge became No. 11. Since its inception in 1926, Corner Brook Lodge has sponsored the formation of Cabot Lodge #13 (formerly #6177), Port aux Basques; Westgate Lodge #18 (formerly #7085), Corner Brook; and Can-Am Lodge #19 (formerly #7219) at Stephenville.

If it can be said that Corner Brook Lodge has an objective, it is “making good men better.” As a fraternity we support our members in their service to their families, their community and their Lodge. Our ladies, especially, are a very important part of all our activities.

Corner Brook Lodge is a vibrant and dynamic Lodge and meets regularly on the first Wednesday of every month (September to June) and on the first Thursday in January at Bennett Hall on West Street, Corner Brook.

Have a look through our website and if you require any further information, please do not hesitate to contact us.

Fraternally;

C. Conrad Mullins
